Our Story

What started as a local hobby quickly expanded into an city-wide business as our realization of the people's needs arose. As our business expanded, so did our customer base, looking for trustworthy and reliable sitters to take care of their children. In 2016, we expanded from the state of Minnesota to Iowa. Then in 2019, we increased our coverage to North Dakota.

Today, we find ourselves in five states, serving over 10,000 users a week. As we look to expand ourselves further, so do we seek to increase our services and reliability to you, the parent. In an unreliable world, leave your children with us where you know they will be taken care of safely and securely.
